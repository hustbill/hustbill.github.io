Watch movies, TV, music, and comics for your computer or mobile devices. All titles are available with no holds and no waiting. Limit of 10 checkouts each month.

Kanopy

Watch thousands of films from independent filmmakers and popular producers. Titles include The Criterion Collection, The Great Courses, and PBS. Also available for Roku.

Kanopy Kids

Watch a streaming collection of age-appropriate films and TV series selected for kids. Finding the right video is easy using Common Sense Media ratings. Titles selected help children to develop empathy, mindfulness, and self-esteem. Set up a personalized PIN to keep your kids inside Kanopy Kids.

Watch free documentary features and series. Available to screen in the classroom or from home.

Explorers sounds like a great choice for an 11-year-old boy who enjoys sci-fi and adventure! Here's why:

Age-appropriate: The movie doesn't have a high rating and is generally considered suitable for pre-teens.
Themes: It celebrates friendship, imagination, and the power of believing in yourself.
Space Adventure: The focus on building a spaceship and exploring another galaxy is likely to be exciting for an 11-year-old boy.

There are a few things to keep in mind:

Made in the 80s: The special effects might seem dated compared to modern movies.
Mild Peril: There are some chase scenes and tense moments, but nothing overly scary.
Here's what you can do to make the decision:

Read Reviews: 
Look for reviews that mention parents' perspectives on the movie's content.
Watch a Trailer: Trailers can give you a good sense of the movie's tone and visuals.

Overall, Explorers is a classic sci-fi adventure that's perfect for an 11-year-old boy with a curious mind and a taste for space exploration.
