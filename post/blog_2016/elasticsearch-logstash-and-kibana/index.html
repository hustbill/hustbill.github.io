ElasticSearch, Logstash and Kibana

2016-03-21
个人笔记

Contents

    • ELK setting in CentOS

The ELK Stack is the combination of Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana that is used specifically in log analytics. Logstash ships log data to Elasticsearch, which indexes the information in a searchable datastore. Kibana then takes the datastore and shows the information in graphical format for log analysis. Logstash is developed by Ruby, Kibana by javascript, Elasticsearch by Java
